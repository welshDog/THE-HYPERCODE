## If Something Goes Wrong

### You Experienced Harm
1. **You're safe here.** We believe you.
2. **Document it** (screenshot/note what happened)
3. **Report it** to conduct@hypercode.dev
4. **We'll respond** within 48 hours
5. **We'll take action** (always)

### We'll Handle:
- Private, confidential conversations
- No retaliation, ever
- Clear next steps
- Your choice of resolution

### Examples of When to Report
- Someone said something discriminatory
- Harassment or bullying happened
- Gatekeeping or exclusion
- Ableist language
- Any behavior that made you feel unsafe
