## ğŸ“‹ Code of Conduct

### Core Values
1. **Neurodiversity is strength, not deficit**
   - No "high-functioning" gatekeeping
   - All cognitive styles welcome
   - Accessibility = everyone benefits

2. **Async over sync, written over spoken**
   - No meetings required to participate
   - Decisions documented in writing
   - Async-first tools (GitHub, Discord threads, email)

3. **Inclusion is ongoing work**
   - Recruit neurodivergent contributors actively
   - Pay them fairly (bounties, grants, sponsorships)
   - Center their voices (not just "diverse team photos")

4. **Science, not politics**
   - Evidence-driven design decisions
   - Reproducible research
   - Honest about failures and limitations

   - Credit is shared
   - Failures are learning
   - Burnout is unacceptable
