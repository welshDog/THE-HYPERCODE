## â“ FAQ

**Q: Do I need to know Python/JavaScript?**  
A: Nope! Beginners welcome. We're testing if HyperCode is easier to learn.

**Q: What if I find a critical bug?**  
A: Report it immediately in Discord. We'll fix it ASAP.

**Q: Can I beta test anonymously?**  
A: Yes! Use a pseudonym, we'll respect your privacy.

**Q: What if I can't finish the 2 weeks?**  
A: Life happens! Just let us know. Partial feedback is still valuable.

**Q: Can I share HyperCode publicly?**  
A: During beta, keep it in our private group. After launch (Dec 15), shout from the rooftops!

**Q: Will this work on my [OS]?**  
A: We support Windows, macOS, and Linux. If it doesn't work, that's important feedback!

**Q: I'm not neurodivergent, can I still apply?**  
A: Yes! We want diverse perspectives. Neurodivergent testers get priority, but everyone's welcome.

**Q: What if I don't like HyperCode?**  
A: Tell us why! Honest criticism is more valuable than fake praise.
